WHO ARE WE?

ABOUT US

Our mission is to spread our love of the sport of Taekwondo through the University of Toronto community. We run registered classes for people of all skill levels, from beginners to black belts! We strive to provide a safe and fun environment for everyone to practice and refine their skills. We also have a competitive team that competes in competitions! We also get together outside of training at our many socials, such as Pub Nights, Beach day, our annual trip to Hart House Farm, and more. Joining is a great way to meet new people and make friends! Improve yourself physically, mentally and socially.

OUR HISTORY

Originally the Trinity College Taekwondo Club, the club soon expanded to become campus wide. The University of Toronto Taekwondo Organization (UTTO) was created in 2006 by a group of Taekwondo enthusiasts and practitioners. Before the club, there had not been any real or proper attempt to offer a qualified level of instruction to the students. Accordingly, the interest in Taekwondo was lost and not accommodated. The founding group members realized that there lacked a dedicated club that seriously understood or demonstrated the physical and mental discipline of the sport and art. Thus, with such thoughts in mind, the founding members of UTTO strove to offer a strong focus on safety, self-defense, fitness, physical self-control, and mental discipline to all club members.

ABOUT TAEKWONDO

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art. In addition to being the national sport of South Korea, it is regarded as the worldâ€™s most popular martial art, if measured by the number of practitioners. Taekwondo is currently well known for its dynamic and fast kicking techniques, although it also involves a variety of other hand techniques as well. There are two styles of Taekwondo. The majority of UTTO practices the style which originates from the Kukkiwon and is governed by World Taekwondo Federation (WTF). The other martial art style is governed by the International Taekwondo Federation (ITF). The two styles are technically and doctrinally different, but still retain many similarities.

FAQ

What style of taekwondo does UTTO practice?

We practice WTF (World Taekwondo Federation) style.

When and where are classes held?

Classes are held at Hart House. For more information on scheduling, check out the Classes page.

Is it possible to try out the class?

Yes! If you are unsure about signing up, you are welcome to have a free trial class. Just come by to one of our scheduled classes.

How do I sign up for classes?

Register at the Hart House front desk or online at their website.

How much do classes cost?

Classes are paid for per semester. To find out the cost for a class, look them up on the Hart House website.

What competitions does UTTO participate in?

We compete in the OUTCL (Ontario University Taekwondo Competitive League) and were the OUTCL collegiate university champions in 2009. We also participate in OTA (Ontario Taekwondo Association) tournaments.
