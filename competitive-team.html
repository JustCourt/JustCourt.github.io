COMPETITIVE TEAM

UTTO's competitive team represents the University of Toronto by competing at local tournaments sanctioned by the Ontario Taekwondo Association. The competitive team is open to ALL LEVELS of athletes ranging from beginners to black belts!

If you are interested in competing, feel free to drop by any of the competitive team practices listed below!
