EVENTS

UTTO regularly hosts social events to give our members a chance to meet and have fun outside of Taekwondo!

Every year we hae our annual Beach Day and trip to Hart House Farm. In the past, we have also hosted socials such as Trampoline Dodgeball and Bubble Soccer. Spots fill up fast so stay on the lookout for announcements for new events! We will also be hosting pub nights, movie nights and other casual socials. More information will be announced on our Facebook page. We hope to see you all there!

Beach Day 2015

Join us for our annual UTTO Beach Day at Centre Island! You can look forward to an exciting day with your fellow UTTO members barbecuing, playing sports, having water gun fights, or just enjoying the sun!
